Health Information

Explore essential health topics, treatments, prevention tips, and more for better health.

Overview of Health Topics

  • Diabetes: Managing blood sugar and related complications.
  • Heart Health: Preventing and managing heart disease.
  • Mental Health: Coping with stress, anxiety, and more.

Symptoms and Diagnosis

  • Diabetes: Excessive thirst, fatigue, diagnosed via glucose tests.
  • Heart Disease: Chest pain, diagnosed through EKGs, blood tests.
  • Mental Health: Mood swings, evaluated with psychological screenings.

Treatment Options

  • Diabetes: Insulin, medications, lifestyle changes.
  • Heart Disease: Medications, surgeries, lifestyle changes.
  • Mental Health: Therapy, medication, cognitive-behavioral therapy.

Prevention Tips

  • Diabetes: Healthy diet, exercise, monitor sugar levels.
  • Heart Disease: Balanced diet, exercise, avoid smoking.
  • Mental Health: Mindfulness, physical activity, seek support.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • Severe or persistent symptoms like chest pain.
  • Significant health changes like sudden weight loss.
  • Mental health issues affecting daily life.
